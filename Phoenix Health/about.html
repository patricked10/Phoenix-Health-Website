Who are we?

Phoenix Health Insurance, a member of the Phoenix Group, is a private commercial health insurance scheme established in 2013 under Section 63(1) of the National Health Insurance Act, 2012 (Act 852). We administer healthcare financing plans and packages that provide financial protection against health risks. The Scheme enrolls individuals, families and staff of corporate bodies, groups and institutions onto a broad healthcare plan that addresses both primary and specialist healthcare needs. We provide tailor-made medical coverage with flexible payment options to scheme members, with special emphasis on preventive health. The backbone of this health insurance scheme is information and communication technology with e-health at the heart of Scheme’s operations. As a new-age healthcare financing company, Phoenix Health Insurance seeks to set higher standards in the health insurance industry, and to be the preferred private healthcare financing plan in Ghana, Africa and the rest of the world.

Phoenix Health Insurance was established in 2013 with the aim to revolutionize the heath insurance industry in Ghana. We had the aim to create a truly memorable healthcare experience by providing tailor-made medical coverage with flexible payment options to scheme members.

We believe every client is unique and deserves all the most attention to details on their needs even before they fall ill. Therefore, we specialize in preventive healthcare. Our hallmark is quality and our providers have been tested and proved to be able to deliver the utmost quality.

Our story is a simple one, we hope to get into the lives of people of all genders, race, ethnicities and ensure that their basic and fundamental component of life – their health is our priority.
