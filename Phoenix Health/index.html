What Makes Us Different?

Flexible Health Plans

Members can choose from our well packaged health plans which ensure all round care for essential and specialist medical services while providing the options to add some benefits such as annual medical screenings, family planning services, herbal treatment and chiropractic services which are ordinarily excluded from general health insurance plans.

Quality Assurance

We have over 400 service provider across all the 16 regions of the country. We also have arrangements with public and mission hospitals to provide services to our members through 5,000 health centers, polyclinics and hospitals.

Delightful Customer Experience

We are available around-the-clock to assist you with any operational challenges you may have in accessing care at any of our contracted health service provider sites. You can contact us 24 hours a day, 7 days in a week.
